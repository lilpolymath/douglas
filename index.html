Profile

Full Version

Jude is deeply rooted in the Nigerian Tech Ecosystem as a result of his consistent innovations and achievements of his startups. He has consistently been recognised every year in local and international tech press/media publications for one achievement or the other since he began his career in tech 8 years ago. He has been entrenched in tech from the early age of 16, getting his first professional role at the age of 19.

He graduated from the University of Lagos with a Bachelor's degree in computer science. After which has worked for a number of companies both home and abroad. A few examples are Sterling bank, Comflo, Africave, Arist etc.

He also is one of the foremost blockchain and web3 leaders in africa, with about 5 years in that space, having worked for some of the biggest names in Defi, like DexAg, Defi Pulse, EPNS, Teller finance.

Jude is the CEO/Cofounder of GetEquity, a company he launched over a year ago.

He is also a venture partner at wefunder.
